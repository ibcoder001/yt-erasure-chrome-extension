YT Erasure

This extension is born out of my OCD which cannot accept the existence of filled up 'Liked Videos Playlist', 'Watch Later Playlist', and Channel subscription after a certain period of time and length.

The extension currently does the following tasks:

  • Deletes all the "Liked playlist videos" one by one. Requires you to navigate to the liked video playlist page by clicking on the link available in extension.
  • Does the same for watched later video playlist.
  • Unsubsribe all the channels in bulk.
